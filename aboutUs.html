About Yuva Marathi

Yuva Marathi is a non-profit organization established for the sole purpose of providing stage to students from Maharashtra to showcase their talents and spread culture in South of India. We host many events and classroom activities throughout the calendar year. 'Aikya' the event in Riviera where all states showcase their culture and our flagship event on 'Gudhipadwa' are well known. Yuva Marathi is indeed 'a family away from home'.
